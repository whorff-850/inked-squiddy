At inked squiddy company we first start by disassembling the squiddy.

After that we start to sand every pvc-c part with a fine grit sandpaper.

We do this part by hand to ensure top quality & no damage to the product.

By sanding the pvc-c the acrylic paint will stick better and look better.

After sanding, we paint each part with high quality acrylic by hand.

After Re-Assembly we ship every inked squiddy in the original box.
